Cycle to Korea

I embarked on a cycling journey, tracing the historic Silk Road from June 2nd, 2018 when I set out from Shanghai, until February 2nd, 2019, when I reached London. This odyssey spanned 14 diverse countries, including China, Kazakhstan, Azerbaijan, Georgia, Turkey, Greece, Macedonia, Albania, Montenegro, Croatia, Italy, Switzerland, France, the U.K., and South Korea.


Throughout this voyage, I conducted in-depth interviews with startup entrepreneurs and delivered seminars, leveraging my expertise as a seasoned tech reporter. I shared knowledge and inspiration while fostering connections across borders.


My passion and experiences found their way into the creation of two books. Cycling the Silk Road and Cycling across the Eurasia and Letters on the Road stand as testaments to the adventures, stories, and knowledge I gained during this expedition.
